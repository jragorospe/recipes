ROASTED TOMATO SOUP

4 SERVINGS
50 MINUTES

INGREDIENTS

  • 6-8 Roma Tomatoes
  • 1/2 Yellow Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Coarse Sea Salt
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 2-3 Cups Vegetable Broth
  • Heavy Cream
  • Fresh Basil
  • Dried Parsley

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
    Line baking sheet with silicone mat or parchment paper.
  2. Cut tomatoes and into halves and seperate onion into layers.
  3. Add tomatoes, onion pieces and garlic to the baking sheet.
    Lightly drizzle with olive oil and add salt to everything.
  4. Bake in the oven for 30-45 minutes or until tomatoes are roasted.
  5. In the meantime, bring chicken broth, basil and parsley to a boil in a pot.
  6. Add tomatoes, onions, garlic and broth to a blender and blend until smooth.
  7. Return to pot. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Ladle into a bowl and add cream, fresh basil and parsley to garnish.
    Eat alongside your favourite grilled cheese recipe and enjoy!
