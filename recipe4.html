SPICY TUSCAN SAUSAGE SOUP

6 SERVINGS
30 MINUTES

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 Onion, Diced
  • 3 Hot Italian Sausages
  • 5 Potatoes
  • 4 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 1 Cup Heavy Cream (or 1.5 Cups Half & Half)
  • 1 Bunch of Kale
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Remove the casings from the sausage and add to a pot until browned.
    Then remove from pot and set aside.
  2. Dice the onion and saute until golden.
  3. Cube potatoes and add both potatoes and broth to the pot.
    Bring to boil and allow potatoes to fully cook.
  4. Add cream and kale to the pot.
    Keep heat on until the cream is warm and kale is wilted.
  5. Add sausage back in and adjust for salt and pepper.
  6. Serve warm with crusty bread and enjoy!
