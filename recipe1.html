SPICY THAI SEAFOOD SOUP

4 SERVINGS
30 MINUTES

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 Tbsp Spicy Thai Paste
  • 1 tsp Fish Sauce
  • 1.5 Cups Chicken Broth
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 Can Coconut Milk
  • 1 Filet Frozen Cod
  • 1 Package Frozen Mussles
  • Butter
  • Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • Red Cabbage
  • Lime

DIRECTIONS

  1. Mince the garlic and add to a pot with butter.
  2. Cook garlic until browned and add spicy thai paste.
  3. Add chicken broth, water, mussles and cubbed cod to pot.
  4. Let simmer for 20+ minutes.
    The longer the steep, the more flavourful the broth.
  5. Add coconut milk and mix.
    (Opt.) Add butter, salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Laddle hot soup into bowl.
    Garnish with cilantro, cabbage, onion and lime and enjoy!
