MISO SOUP

8 SERVINGS
120 MINUTES

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 g Konbu
  • 3 L Water
  • 40 g Bonito
  • White Miso (to taste)
  • Semi-firm Tofu
  • Green Onion
  • Butter (optional)

DIRECTIONS

MAKE THE DASHI
  1. Soak konbu in cold water for 1 hour.
  2. Bring water to just bellow a simmer (85 degrees C).
    This prevents the dashi from ever becoming bitter.
  3. Once the water is at temp, remove konbu.
  4. Add the bonito all at once and remove from heat.
  5. Steep bonito until it settles and then strain.
    This can take up to 30 minutes.
FINISHING THE MISO SOUP
  1. Add white miso and taste. Add more to your liking.
    (Opt.) Add butter to the stock to give it extra flavour.
  2. Cut tofu into small cubes and place in the bottom of a bowl.
  3. Chop green onions and add to bowl.
  4. Ladle in hot miso soup and enjoy!
